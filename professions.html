Companies and their professions

Bethesda Game Studios is a video game company from the USA. The studios are owned by Zenimax Media. Bethesda mostly uses c++. Bethesda is well known for their Elder Scrolls franchise, DOOM games series, Fallout series and a lot more. Bethesda works Agile.


Spil Games is a company in the Netherlands. Spil Games has created a lot of free-to-play games for children, both on mobile and through Adobe Flash. They use ActionScript for their flash games, and C# in their mobile games. Spil Games works Agile.


Electronic Arts, more commonly known as EA, is a very popular video game company. They are the creators of FIFA, Need for Speed, The Sims and a lot more games. EA works with C++ and C#, the former for runtime and the latter for the editor. They work with the TOGAF Architecture development method.

Sony is a Japanese electronics company. They are well-known for their televisions and monitors, as well as their Playstation console series. Most Playstation games use C++. Sony's process is based on the SCRUM approach.

Apple is a multinational company originally from the USA. They develop various hardware, such as the iPhone and iMac, as well as various software, like iOS. Apple programs their software in Swift, a programming language they created themselves. They work Agile, but also with Test Driven Development approach and in some projects SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) is followed as well.

Macaw is a company from the Netherlands that delivers digital solutions to customers problems, to help them feel successful. To do this, they use a lot of the latest software, first-class technology and the best people. Macaw works Agile, or with the Waterfall method.

YourSurprise is a company from the Netherlands that sells personalized gifts. Their website is mostly made out of HTML and Javascript. YourSurprise works Agile.

VeraCode is a company from the United States that focuses on application and/or website security, e.g. preventing breaches and leaks. They do so by "manual penetration testing", which means they test out how strong their security is by simulating a cyberattack. VeraCode works with Javascript, C++ and SQL, and they follow the SDLC approach.
